Isaac Swanson

trumpeter

Isaac P. Swanson is currently the Repiano Cornetist in the Dallas Brass Band, trumpet player in the Brazos Chamber Orchestra and plays in Salsa Bands and Big Bands in the DFW area. Mr. Swanson is also a trumpet teacher at Eagle Mountain-Saginaw ISD and Keller ISD where he has a large competitive trumpet studio. Formerly, he has held positions as the Assistant Band Director and Jazz Director at Lubbock-Cooper Middle School, Instructor of Trumpet and Jazz Band at Lubbock Christian University and Trumpet Instructor at Laramie County Community College. Mr. Swanson built a reputation as having some of the most unique and innovative ways to teaching trumpet and brass to all grade levels and abilities. Mr. Swanson's students have competed at the region, area and All-State levels; received scholarships into various colleges, earned graduate assistantships into universities and some have played in various drum and bugle corps. Mr. Swanson has directed Jazz Ensembles, Latin Jazz Ensembles, Secondary Concert Bands and Chamber groups. He learned lead trumpet playing from what many would consider some of the best lead trumpet players of our time including Bobby Shew, Roger Ingram, Brian MacDonald, Brad Steinwehe, Jon Faddis and Wayne Bergeron. In addition to lead trumpet players, Isaac has studied with Professor Will Strieder, Bill Pfund, Dr. Robert Murray, Dr. Scott Meredith. Isaac has shared the stage with Bobby Shew, Wayne Bergeron, Dee Dee Bridgewater, Bill Holman, Kansas, Charlie Aponte, Patty Padilla, Jordan Kahn Orchestra, Elmer Churampi, The Eric Reed Trio, The O'Jays, Tito Puente, Glenn Kostur, Ron Wilkins, Conjunto Colores, Bill Watrous, Dave Douglas, John Riley, Mario Pesacreta, Jim White, the Lubbock Symphony Orchestra and Mannheim Steamroller. Mr. Swanson has collaborated with various High Schools and College Big Bands as a performer, director and clinician. He recorded UNC Lab Band I in 1998-1999 with Gene Aitken on the album Alive XVII “For The Last Time!”

Mr. Swanson holds degrees from Aims Community College, University of Northern Colorado, University of Wyoming and is a Doctoral Candidate at Texas Tech University studying trumpet exclusively with Professor Will Strieder and Jazz with Professor Stephen Jones.

Mr. Swanson is a source on Mr. Strieder’s methods and is a top source on Free-Buzzing Methods for brass and trumpet players.

Isaac P. Swanson is a Carol Brass Artist and Clinician
