Isaac Swanson

trumpeter

Isaac P. Swanson is currently Principal Trumpet in the Sherman Symphony Orchestra, Substitute Trumpet in the Lubbock Symphony Orchestra and previous played Principal Trumpet in the Brazos Chamber Orchestra. Mr. Swanson’s versatility extends to playing in Brass Quintets, Salsa Bands and Big Bands in the DFW area. Mr. Swanson is also the Trumpet Instructor at Midwestern State University, Austin College, Eagle Mountain-Saginaw ISD and Keller ISD and also teaches students from HEB ISD, Granbury ISD and Weatherford ISD where he has a large competitive trumpet studio. Formerly, he held positions as the Assistant Band Director and Jazz Director at Lubbock-Cooper Middle School, Instructor of Trumpet and Jazz Band at Lubbock Christian University and Trumpet Instructor at Laramie County Community College. Mr. Swanson built a reputation as having some of the most unique and innovative ways to teach trumpet and brass to all grade levels and abilities. Mr. Swanson has taught trumpet players who have made All-State Band and Jazz Bands in Texas, Colorado, Wyoming and New Mexico. Many students have received scholarships into various colleges, earned graduate assistantships into universities and some have played in various drum and bugle corps. Mr. Swanson has directed Jazz Ensembles, Latin Jazz Ensembles, Concert Bands and Chamber groups. He has learned lead trumpet playing from what many would consider some of the best lead trumpet players of our time including Bobby Shew, Roger Ingram, Brian MacDonald, Brad Steinwehe, Jon Faddis and Wayne Bergeron. In addition to lead trumpet players, Isaac has studied with Professor Will Strieder, Bill Pfund, Dr. Robert Murray, Dr. Scott Meredith. Isaac has shared the stage with Bobby Shew, Wayne Bergeron, Dee Dee Bridgewater, Bill Holman, Kansas, Charlie Aponte, Patty Padilla, Jordan Kahn Orchestra, Elmer Churampi, The Eric Reed Trio, The O'Jays, Tito Puente, Glenn Kostur, Ron Wilkins, Conjunto Colores, Bill Watrous, Dave Douglas, John Riley, Mario Pesacreta, Jim White, the Lubbock Symphony Orchestra, Sherman Symphony Orchestra, Brazos Chamber Orchestra, Dallas Brass Band and Mannheim Steamroller. Mr. Swanson has collaborated with various High Schools and College Big Bands as a performer, director and clinician. He recorded UNC Jazz Lab Band I in 1998-1999 with Gene Aitken on the album Alive XVII “For The Last Time!”

Mr. Swanson holds degrees from Aims Community College, University of Northern Colorado, University of Wyoming and is a Doctoral Candidate at Texas Tech University studying trumpet exclusively with Professor Will Strieder and Jazz with Professor Stephen Jones.

Mr. Swanson is a source on Mr. Strieder’s methods and is a top source on Free-Buzzing Methods for brass and trumpet players.

Isaac P. Swanson is a Carol Brass Artist and Clinician
